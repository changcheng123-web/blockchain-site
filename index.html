什么是区块链？ / What is Blockchain?

区块链是一种去中心化的分布式账本技术，通过密码学和共识机制确保数据不可篡改、全网可验证。它正重塑金融、供应链、身份验证、游戏和更多行业。

Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed ledger technology that enables secure, transparent and tamper-proof record-keeping across a network. It’s revolutionizing finance, supply chain, identity, gaming and more.
